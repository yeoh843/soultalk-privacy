Data Deletion Request

How to Delete Your Data

You can delete your account and all associated data in two ways:

Option 1: Delete from App

  1. Open SoulTalk app
  2. Go to Settings
  3. Tap Delete Account
  4. Confirm deletion

Option 2: Request via Email

  1. Send email to: privacy@soultalk.app
  2. Subject: "Data Deletion Request"
  3. Include your email address used for registration
  4. We will process your request within 30 days

What Gets Deleted

  • Your account information
  • All chat conversations
  • Mood tracking data
  • Profile information
  • All associated data

Note: Data deletion is permanent and cannot be undone.

Processing Time

Data deletion requests are processed within 30 days of receipt.

Questions? Email: privacy@soultalk.app
